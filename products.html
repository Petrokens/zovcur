Therapeutic Segments

Specialized healthcare solutions for diverse medical needs

Gynecology & Women's Health

Comprehensive solutions for women's health, including therapies for reproductive care, hormonal support, pregnancy wellness, fertility management, and post-menopausal care.

Cardiology

Cardiovascular therapies ranging from antihypertensives, lipid-lowering agents, and antiplatelets to advanced cardiac care solutions, addressing both preventive and therapeutic needs.

Diabetology & Endocrinology

A full spectrum of anti-diabetic medications and supportive therapies to help manage blood glucose levels, improve insulin sensitivity, and enhance the overall quality of life for diabetic patients.

Oncology

Supportive care products for cancer patients, including pain management, nutritional support, immunity enhancers, and therapies to reduce treatment-related side effects.

Neurology & Psychiatry

Neurological care products designed for the management of epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, neuropathic pain, migraines, and other central nervous system disorders.

Gastroenterology

A wide portfolio addressing gastrointestinal health, including acid reducers, digestive enzymes, probiotics, and medicines for liver and intestinal health.

Pulmonology

Innovative respiratory care therapies for conditions such as asthma, COPD, bronchitis, and seasonal allergies, ensuring better breathing and improved lung function.

Nephrology

Specialized products for kidney health and renal conditions, supporting patients with chronic kidney disease and related disorders.

Orthopedics & Pain Management

Formulations that support bone and joint health, including pain management solutions, anti-inflammatory drugs, osteoporosis treatments, and nutritional supplements for mobility and strength.

Pediatrics

Safe, effective, and child-friendly formulations for pediatric care, addressing common childhood illnesses, nutritional support, growth, and immunity.

Dermatology & Cosmetology

Comprehensive dermatological range including antifungals, antibacterials, emollients, acne care, and skin protection therapies tailored to improve skin health.

Infectious Diseases

Advanced antimicrobial therapies for bacterial, viral, fungal, and parasitic infections, supporting global public health initiatives.

Critical Care

Specialized medications for emergency and intensive care settings, providing vital support for critically ill patients.

Urology

Urological medicines designed for kidney stone management, urinary tract infections, prostate health, and other male and female urinary conditions.

Geriatrics

Comprehensive healthcare solutions designed for the elderly, focusing on chronic disease management, mobility, bone strength, cognitive health, and overall wellness.
